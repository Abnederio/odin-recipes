Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is both a type of wide, flat pasta and a popular baked dish made by layering

this pasta with other ingredients, such as sauces (like tomato-based ragù or béchamel), cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan)

, and fillings like meat or vegetables. It is an Italian dish that is baked in an oven until the top is golden and the layers are tender and bubbly.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles (dry or fresh)
  • Meat and sauce
  • Cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Seasonings like salt, pepper, oregano, and basil

Steps:

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  3. Cook the Noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Layer the Ingredients
  6. Bake
  7. Rest

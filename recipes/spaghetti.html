Spaghetti

Description:

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta made from durum wheat, water, and sometimes eggs.

The name comes from the Italian word spaghetto, meaning "little string". It is a staple food in Italian cuisine

and is most commonly served with a variety of sauces, such as tomato sauce with meatballs or a meat-based Bolognese.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce/purée/paste
  • Aromatics
  • Seasonings
  • Olive oil
  • Grated hard cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Salt
  3. Add Spaghetti
  4. Drain
  5. Brown the Meat
  6. Sauté Aromatics
  7. Combine and Serve
